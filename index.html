About Us

Skoom Coffee is a café born from countless morning coffees shared between us—in the great outdoors, cafés, car rides and more. We're a husband and wife team's love letter to coffee and community. Nestled in a heritage building in downtown Toronto, we're here to provide a moment for yourself or with friends over excellent coffee and fresh pastries made to be the coffee you love. We're glad you're here.
